About My Goals

The four goals I created are essential for building a sustainable life. They emphasize responsible resource use, environmental awareness, and social responsibility. By implementing these goals, we can ensure a balanced approach to meeting today's needs without compromising the ability of future generations to thrive.

Project Description

This project represents two United Nations Sustainable Development Goals through code-based graphics.

On the left side, "Sustainable Energy for All" is shown with a gold background. A white star and golden circle symbolize renewable solar energy, while geometric lines represent infrastructure for efficient energy distribution.

On the right side, "Good Health and Well-Being" is displayed with a forest green background. A crimson circle with a white medical cross highlights healthcare access and the universal symbol of health services.

This artwork demonstrates how coding and design can visually communicate important global goals, encouraging awareness and action toward a more sustainable future.
